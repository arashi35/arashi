<h4 id="〜だけ">〜だけ</h4>
<p>An auxiliary particle to express exclusiveness. e.g.,</p>
<blockquote>
<p>あなた<strong>だけ</strong>が知っているでしょう！<br>
(只有你知道吧！)</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Also, it is used to express the extend of the event, e.g.,</p>
<blockquote>
<p>あれ<strong>だけ</strong>の…<br>
([就算]如此的…)</p>
</blockquote>
